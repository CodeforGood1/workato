Project Overview

WorkChainX is a modular, AI-augmented workflow suite designed for modern manufacturing environments. Built using Workato, it integrates real-time factory floor data with enterprise applications, external intelligence, and AI services to proactively manage operations, reduce downtime, and improve decision-making.

Smart Inventory Replenisher 2.0 - Our Flagship Feature

When stock of a critical item drops below threshold, our AI system pulls approved vendors from ERP, uses OpenAI and external data (news, social, climate, geopolitical alerts) to assess disruption risk for each supplier, scores suppliers based on cost, lead time, quality, carbon impact, and risk, then recommends optimal vendor and creates purchase orders automatically.
